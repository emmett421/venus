Venus' features

  • Real Color

    In a real color image of Venus it is white and smooth.

  • Alternate Images

    When Venus us captured using different types of image sensors, it can have a different appearance. This is why Venus appears differently in many different photos because it is often captured differently.

  • Surface Composition

    A number of Soviet landers analyze the chemical composition of the surface materials of Venus. The rock compostion may be similar to granite or igneous rocks which cover the Earth's continents.

  • Elements

    X-ray instruments have measured concentrations of silicon, aluminium, magnesium, iron, calcium, potassium, titanium, manganese, and sulfur on Venus.

  • Moons

    Venus has no moons at all like Mercury.

  • Atmosphere

    Venus' atmosphere is composed primarily of carbon dioxide and is much denser and hotter than Earth.
