What would it be like to live on Venus? Can you?

Living on Venus

Venus is neighbors with the Earth so it would seem like they would be similar. That isn't really true. The atmosphere of Venus is so hot and thick you wouldn't be able to breath it. Because the atmosphere has a lot of carbon dioxide in it, clouds of CO2 trap energy from the sun and release it as heat. The surface temperature is hot enough to melt lead.

It would be extremely difficult for a human to overcome these challenges even with structures to support them. It is unlikely many advancements will be made to inhabit Venus.

Interesting Facts

    A day on Venus lasts longer than a year.
    Venus rotates in the opposite direction of other planets.
    Venus is the second brightest object in the night sky.
